Privacy Policy

We use the information to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Your photos are processed locally on your device whenever possible or securely in the cloud to generate stamps.

We implement reasonable security measures to help protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through the app settings. You may also export your data upon request.

5. Changes to Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
